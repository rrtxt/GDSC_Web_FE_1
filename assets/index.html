Daftar Makanan

Nasi Goreng

A popular dish made with fried rice, mixed with vegetables and meat, and topped with a fried egg.

Sate

Skewers of marinated meat, usually chicken or beef, grilled and served with a peanut sauce.

<

Gado-Gado

A mixed vegetable salad with a peanut sauce dressing.

Mie Goreng

Fried noodles mixed with vegetables and meat, and topped with a fried egg.

Rendang

A spicy meat stew, usually made with beef or chicken, cooked in coconut milk and a variety of spices.

Sate Lilit

Minced meat skewers made from fish or seafood, mixed with spices and grilled on a skewer.

Bakso

Meatball soup made with beef or chicken, served with noodles, vegetables, and a spicy broth.

Pepes

Fish or seafood wrapped in banana leaves and steamed or grilled.

Soto

A soup made with meat, vegetables, and a variety of spices, served with rice.
